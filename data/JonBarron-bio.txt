Seou Choi is a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science at MIT, where he works on optical computing, quantum optics, and nanophotonics. He received an M.S. in EECS from MIT in 2024 under the supervision of Prof. Marin Soljacic, and a B.S. in Electrical and Computer Engineering from Seoul National University in 2022, graduating summa cum laude. His recent research spans photonic probabilistic machine learning using quantum vacuum noise, quantum-state dynamics in nonlinear optical cavities, and nanophotonic scintillators for X-ray imaging.
